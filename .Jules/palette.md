## 2024-05-18 - [Add aria-label and aria-expanded to mobile menu button]
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons used for state toggles (like a hamburger menu) often miss explicit `aria-label`s and `aria-expanded` attributes, breaking accessibility for screen reader users trying to open menus.
**Action:** Always check icon-only toggle buttons for dynamic `aria-label`s and `aria-expanded` attributes.
